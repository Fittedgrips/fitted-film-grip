About Fitted Film Grip

Fitted Film Grip is a professional Key Grip and Best Boy Grip based in Lagos, Nigeria, providing expert grip, rigging and camera support for feature films, commercials, documentaries, TV productions and music videos.

Working closely with Directors, Cinematographers (DOPs), Camera Operators and Production Teams, I help create safe, efficient and creative camera movement solutions that bring every story to life.

My goal is simple: deliver reliable grip support that allows productions to achieve cinematic visuals safely and professionally.

Professional Grip Services

🎥 Key Grip

Leading grip operations, supervising equipment setup, camera support systems, rigging solutions and on-set safety.

🎬 Best Boy Grip

Supporting the Key Grip with equipment management, crew coordination, rigging preparation and production logistics.

📽 Camera Rigging

Professional mounting solutions, sliders, dollies and creative camera movement setups for every production.

⚙️ On-Set Support

Working alongside production crews to ensure safe, efficient and professional execution on every shoot.
