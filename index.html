WHO I AM

I'm a professional Key Grip & Best Boy Grip working across feature films, commercials, documentaries, music videos and television productions.

My responsibility is to ensure every camera movement is safe, stable and visually precise through expert rigging, camera support and grip equipment.

I collaborate closely with Directors of Photography, Camera Operators and Gaffers to create cinematic visuals that help tell unforgettable stories.
